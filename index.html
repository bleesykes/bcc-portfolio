Hello!

I am a 33 year old Married stay-at-home Mom to a wild almost 2 year old little girl. I graduated from Bristol Community College in 2009 with an Associates Degree in Business Administration. I worked in the banking field for many years after.

I wanted a career change after I had my daughter. With that being said, I am currently attending BCC to pursue an Associate's Degree in Computer Information Systems. I then plan to transfer to a University to obtain a Bachelor's Degree in UX/UI Web Design. I look forward to learning all that I can this semester.

I consider myself to be a VAK (Visual Auditory and Kinesthetic) learner. I am a visual learner, I prefer watching video tutorials that navigate through each step of how to do something rather than sit there and read a text book about it. I have found infographics on topics to also be helpful when learning.

Listening to an audio book versus reading the book myself gives me a better understanding about the topic. Audio books have been great in helping me restart my College journey after 12+ years. Hearing words repeated back in my ear while viewing the text is also very helpful.

Kinesthetic (movement and touch) learning has been a useful tool to me. When I am learning something new in my CIS programming course, it helps me to touch the keyboard and type out examples and see how each code works. I enjoy seeing the trial and error of it all.
